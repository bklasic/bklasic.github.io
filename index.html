ABOUT BRITTNEY KLASIC

CHILDHOOD

Brittney had a love and respect for horses since she could remember. She started riding at Central Arizona Riding Academy in Chandler, AZ with Dorie Vlatten-Schmitz and Uli Schmitz at the age of 9 in Dressage, but also had lessons in jumping and western disciplines which set the importance of cross-training.

DRESSAGE

Brittney went on to compete with the Region 5 Junior Team to win team Gold in the 2008 NAJRC with her horse. Wadi Draa. She then moved to San Diego, CA to train with Steffen and Shannon Peters: where she was able to compete and win her USDF Silver Medal on her horse, Mowgli. While at Arroyo Del Mar, she had the opportunity to ride several different horses, which taught her the importance of developing a soft and supple horse. Her knowledge of horse care. proper maintenance of older horse athletes and veterinary care grew.

KLASIC TRAINING

After graduating from CSU San Marcos with a Bachelor of Chemistry, she started working at Scripps Hospital part time to pursue her passion for teaching and training horse and rider with the creation of Klasic Training. She currently serves Fallbrook, but also offers training and lessons at Far View Farms in San Marcos. CA.

FACILITY

Far View Farms Farms is a 6 acre facility in San Marcos California. Our facility has the following amenities:

  • 3 barns
  • Large dressage court with GGT footing
  • Jump arena with GGT footing
  • Round pen for lunging
  • Smaller arena coming soon
